THE BEAUTY IS IN THE DETAILS

Jacksonville's Certified Balayage Experts

Balayage means to sweep, and unlike foil highlights, this method disguises unwanted re-growth lines by hand painting highlights customized for your exact look — creating a more natural, sophisticated, dimensional finish. Great for beachy blondes, ash blondes, brunettes, creative colors, redheads, and even black hair.
